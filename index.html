The Sudarshan Rangamanch is a theatre organization located in Pune, Maharashtra. It is run by the 'Maharashtra Cultural Center', a leading public charitable trust active in cultural activities. The Institution was established on 5 October 1965. Its past presidents were Smt. Yamutai Kirloskar, Dr. Pabalkar, Smt. Dr. Suru, Smt. Jyotsna Bhole. At present, the well-known actor Dr. Mohan Agashe is heading the institution. The trust has its own mini-theatre as well as an art gallery. Hyderabad prasad's Imax

An IMAX movie theater in Hyderabad is Prasad IMAX . It is a centrally air-conditioned multiplex with an area of ​​2,35,000 square feet. The multiplex has five screens, a food court, multinational fast food outlets, a gaming zone, a shopping mall covering two levels of the complex . It is the most popular IMAX theater in the world . Its screen is 72 feet high and 95 feet wide. The theater has 635 seats with a 12,000 watt sound system. It is the largest IMAX 3D screen in the world, with the Sydney IMAX Theater (123 x 97 ft) having the largest IMAX screen.
